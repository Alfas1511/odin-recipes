Chicken Alfredo

Description

Chicken Alfredo is a creamy Italian-American pasta dish made with tender pieces of chicken tossed in a rich, buttery Alfredo sauce. The sauce is made with butter, heavy cream, and Parmesan cheese, creating a silky coating that pairs perfectly with fettuccine noodles.

This hearty and satisfying dish is perfect for a comforting dinner, combining the richness of the sauce with the savory flavor of grilled or sautéed chicken.

Ingredients

  • 200g fettuccine pasta
  • 2 chicken breasts, sliced thinly
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Steps for preparation

  1. Cook the Pasta

    1. Boil fettuccine in salted water until al dente.
    2. Drain and set aside.

  2. Cook the Chicken

    1. Season chicken with salt and pepper.
    2. In a skillet, heat olive oil and cook chicken until golden and cooked through. Remove and set aside.

  3. Prepare the Alfredo Sauce

    1. In the same skillet, melt butter and sauté garlic until fragrant.
    2. Add heavy cream and simmer gently for 2–3 minutes.
    3. Stir in Parmesan cheese until melted and smooth.

  4. Combine Pasta and Chicken

    1. Add the cooked pasta and chicken to the sauce.
    2. Toss well until evenly coated.

  5. Serve

    1. Garnish with chopped parsley and extra Parmesan.
    2. Serve warm and enjoy!
